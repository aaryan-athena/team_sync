What We Offer

Everything You Need to Analyze Your Game

From automatic shot detection to frame-perfect basket tracking — Hoop Sync handles the analysis so you can focus on improving.

🎯
Shot Detection
YOLOv11 identifies "Player Shooting" posture with a custom confidence threshold, counting each attempt without double-counting thanks to a 1.5-second cooldown system.
🏀
Basket Tracking
Detects "Ball in Basket" events with a 2-second cooldown to prevent false positives. If a basket is scored without a detected shot, one is automatically added.
📊
Live Statistics
Real-time HUD overlay showing shots attempted, baskets made, and shooting accuracy percentage — updated every frame as the video is processed.
⚡
Three Analysis Modes
Choose Full Tracking (bounding boxes + HUD), Stats & Effects (clean view with animations), or Stats Only (minimal overlay). Each mode fits a different use case.
🎛️
Custom Thresholds
Adjust confidence thresholds per detection class in real time. Tuning these for your lighting and camera angle improves detection accuracy significantly.
🧹
Auto Cleanup
Uploaded and processed files are automatically deleted after 30 minutes, keeping the server lean and your footage private without any manual action.

How It Works

Three Steps to Your Analysis

1
Upload Your Footage
Select a basketball game video (up to 3 minutes). Enable Test Mode to preview with just the first 15 seconds.
2
Configure & Start
Pick your processing mode, tweak detection thresholds if needed, then hit Start Analysis. The AI handles everything else.
3
Download & Review
Once complete, download the annotated video with a full performance summary screen at the end — shots, baskets, accuracy, and duration.

Model Performance

Production-Grade Detection Accuracy

The YOLOv11s model was trained on a custom basketball dataset from Roboflow using sports-optimized augmentations including HSV saturation tuning for orange ball detection, shear for camera angle robustness, and mixup for player overlap handling.

🎓 Trained with AdamW optimizer, cosine learning rate decay over 200 epochs on an NVIDIA GTX 1060.
✅ Detects 5 classes: Ball, Ball in Basket, Player, Basket, and Player Shooting.

Processing Modes

Choose Your View Style

Full Tracking
Bounding Boxes + HUD + Effects
Shows all detection boxes (ball, players, basket) in color-coded rectangles with confidence scores, plus the live stats HUD and scoring animations. Best for debugging or coaching sessions where visibility of every detection matters.
Stats & Effects
Clean View + HUD + Animations
Hides bounding boxes but keeps the live stats overlay and the pulsing basket-scoring animation. Great for creating highlight reels or sharing with players who don't need to see raw AI output.
Stats Only
Minimal HUD Overlay
Just the scoreboard — no boxes, no animations. A clean, minimal look for presentations or situations where you want the original footage to remain as unobstructed as possible while still seeing the stats.
