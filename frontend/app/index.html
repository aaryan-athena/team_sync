Tips for Live Analysis

📡
Run Locally
Live feed works best when the server runs on the same machine or local network. Latency depends on inference speed.
📷
Camera Position
Side-court angle, slightly elevated on a tripod. Avoid fisheye lenses or extreme wide angles.
☀️
Good Lighting
Bright, even lighting improves detection. Outdoor courts in daylight produce the best results.
🔒
HTTPS for Production
Browsers require HTTPS to access the camera in production. For local use, http://localhost works fine.
